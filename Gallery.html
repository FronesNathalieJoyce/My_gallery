Welcome my fellow art enthusiasts!

Hi! My name is Nathalie Joyce Frones. Natskie is the name I'm using on my artworks so this is my pen name. My hobby is making art sketches and digital art. Natskie began drawing people at the age of five and learn arts during her studies on Grade School. She has yet to make her works to be profitable as this is her leisure and passion. This website may help her to be known by the people.

Water Color Painting

These paintings are made with using watercolor. These are made during art class as Natskie's project on the subject.

The left painting is titled as "The Woman's hair" and the style of painting is Cubism. The painting on the right is called "The Parasol Lady" and the style is called Realism Art because this is a scene of a Japanese girl strolling on the mountain admiring the Sakura tree.

Digital Arts

Digital art is getting popular these days as some artists are on to computer aided softwares. They can already manipulate animations and CGI effects in their works. Natskie, eventhough is not yet skilled on the foundations of art, is currently studying digital arts. The advantage of this, is that you spend less on art materials, easy to transfer, save, art reuse and is in demand all over the internet. Natskie use the App MediBang Paint on her smartphone because she cannot afford a graphics tablet and a pc. First, she took shots of her sketches then do the editing on the phone.
